Statistics

 

The Joint Strategic Needs Assessment (JSNA) brings together data and intelligence that helps us understand the needs of our local population.

Any queries for the Insight and Intelligence team?

  • Email: research@cheshirewestandchester.gov.uk

Please note: The 2021 Census population estimate for Cheshire West and Chester is 357,200.  This is higher than the Office for National statistics had previously estimated the population (343,800 mid-2020 estimate).  Products on this website including State of the Borough Report, Population Estimates and Population Forecasts were produced before the Census results were published.  Population in all these products should be used with caution as the 2021 Census provides the most accurate estimate of the population.  These products will be updated with the latest census based population estimates over the coming months. 

Ward boundaries in Cheshire West and Chester changed in May 2019. This means that some products on this website produced before May 2019 contain statistics for wards that no longer exist and should be used with caution.
